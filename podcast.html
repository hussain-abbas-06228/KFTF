PODCAST

By: Shahmir Faisal, Zain ul Abiden, Burare Hassan

Rebuilding Karachi · Episode 1 - The Future of Waste Management in Karachi

The first episode of the podcast is a conversation with Ahmed, an employee who works under the Sindh Solid Waste Management Board. The episode revolved around waste management in Karachi. How it works currently, its shortcomings, and what the future holds for waste management in this overpopulated city.


Rebuilding Karachi · Episode 2 - The Future of Water Scarcity in Karachi

The second episode of the podcast is a discussion with Dr. Hassaan Furqan Khan on the issue of water scarcity in Karachi. Being someone who's primary research interests have always been water related, he had much to say about his own projects, along with his opinion on the future of water scarcity in Karachi, all of which is discussed in this episode
