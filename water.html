Karachi could be completely submerged by 2060, warn experts

Karachi gears up to cater to rising sea levels and future scarcity of soft water. Proximity to the sea and underground pollution cause a lack of soft water. People have started using hard water-friendly products including hard water-friendly facewash.



1. HARD-WATER FRIENDLY FACEWASH

Designed by: Swaleha Saleem



DIRECTIONS:

  • Use a gentle, non-abrasive cleaner that does not contain alcohol.

  • Wet your face with lukewarm water and use your fingertips to apply facewash.

  • Rinse your face with lukewarm water and pat dry.

  • Apply moisturizer if you have dry skin.

2. NATIONAL CLOTING CARD

Designed by: Tasbiha Asim



All legal aged Karachi residents are to buy their "new" clothing items using the National Clothing Card (NCC) only. Please acquire one as soon as you reach the legal age.
