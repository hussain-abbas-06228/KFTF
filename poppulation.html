Karavaan Housing Society- A Project by ISPR

Karavaan Housing Society (KHS) is a project by Pakistan's military to tackle the ever-increasing climate crisis in Karachi. The military, in collaboration with the Karachi East District Council, has successfully made it functional in one of the prime locations of the city. Through various environment-friendly strategies, KHS aims to stop resource wastage without compromising the needs of its residents.





Zimaydaar Shehri Card

The Sustainable Green Card is an initiative to keep the residents on track with their green activities. The program brings you with exciting plans and offers that the residents of KHS can access by merely engaging in sustainable activities. From planting a tree to saving resources or taking a business initiative, anything that contributes to the sustainability of the environment can help you earn points. This sustainable card can help you avail incentives in education, health services etc.
